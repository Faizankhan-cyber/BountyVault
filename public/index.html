How It Works

Happy Path

  1. Poster creates bounty with requirements
  2. Workers submit proposals
  3. Poster selects winner
  4. Work delivered and payment released

Dispute Path

  1. Poster or worker raises dispute
  2. Admins review evidence
  3. Decision made by arbitration
  4. Funds distributed accordingly
